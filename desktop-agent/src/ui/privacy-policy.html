🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 | Version 1.0

1. What We Collect

The Productivity Agent collects the following data during work hours:

  • Activity Data: Active and idle periods based on keyboard/mouse input
  • Application Usage: Names of applications used and time spent
  • Website Visits: Domains visited and duration (via browser extension)
  • Screenshots: Periodic screen captures (encrypted at rest)
  • Attendance: Clock-in/out times

2. How We Use Your Data

Data is used solely for:

  • Generating productivity analytics and reports
  • Attendance tracking and workforce management
  • Security monitoring and anomaly detection
  • Improving team workflows and identifying bottlenecks
Important: We do NOT record keystrokes, passwords, personal messages, or any content within applications. We do NOT track activity outside work hours.

3. Data Security

  • All screenshots are encrypted using AES-256 before storage
  • Data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized managers/admins
  • All data access is logged and auditable

4. Your Rights

  • View all data collected about you at any time
  • A visible indicator shows when tracking is active
  • Pause tracking temporarily from the system tray
  • Revoke consent and stop all monitoring
  • Request deletion of your data per retention policy

5. Data Retention

Activity data is retained for 365 days. Screenshots are retained for 90 days. After retention periods, data is automatically and permanently deleted.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact your HR department or email privacy@yourcompany.com.
